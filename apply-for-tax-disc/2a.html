What's a V11 reference number?

It's the 16 digit number on the reminder letter that we send out when your tax disc is due for renewal.

I don't have the reminder

You can still buy your tax disc online using the vehicle's registration number and V5C (log book) number instead.

IMPORTANT: You can't do this if you've recently purchased your vehicle and don't yet have the full V5C (the green slip alone is not enough).
