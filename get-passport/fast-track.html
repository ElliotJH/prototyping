--- layout: get-passport title: How to apply for a passport using Fast-track or Premium services. categories: Quick answer ---

To use Fast Track or Premium service you have to apply in person at your nearest Identity and Passport Service (IPS) centre. Do this by calling the Passport Adviceline on 0300 222 0000

Demand fot appointments is high, so allow up to 2 weeks for your appointment date - you can't book more than 2 weeks in advance.
