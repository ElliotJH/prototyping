ServiceChange the date or location of your practical driving test

It takes about five minutes to make a change to your practical driving test appointment online.

What you need to know before you start

You’ll need 2 of the following:

  • application reference number
  • theory test pass certificate number (find this online)
  • Great Britain or Northern Ireland driving licence number

Remember to check that your driving instructor is available on the new time and date.

You must give at least 3 clear working days’ notice to change your test date or you’ll have to pay again. Sundays and public holidays don’t count as working days.

You can change your appointment 3 times - after that you’ll have to cancel your test and book a new one.
