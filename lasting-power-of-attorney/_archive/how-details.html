What happens next?

After a six week waiting period your LPA will be returned to you registered. It's then ready for use.

Why the wait?

The six weeks is to give people an opportunity to raise any concerns about the LPA (for example, if they think you're being coerced into it, or that a fraud is being committed). You'll be asked when you create your LPA who you'd like to tell about it.
