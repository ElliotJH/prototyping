2. About the attorneys

An attorney is a person appointed by you (the donor) to make all decisions on your behalf when you are unable to make decisions for yourself. An attorney should be a person who knows you well and who you trust to understand your wishes and to make decisions in your best interests. Attorneys must be aged 18 or over.
