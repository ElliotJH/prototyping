Before you start...

Before you can use this service you'll need to confirm your email address and set a password so you can save your progress. If you've already done this, please sign in here.

Please note, a lasting power of attorney is governed by the laws of England and Wales and can only be made for someone who is over 18 and who currently has mental capacity.

If you're making one on behalf of someone else then you must also be over 18.
