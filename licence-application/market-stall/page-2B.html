Apply for a market stall licence
from London Borough of Westminster

It's a three step process:

  1. Download the application form

    Right-click the link below and select the 'Save' option

    Download the application form (PDF)

  2. Fill in the application form on your computer

    To edit the application form you'll need a recent version of Adobe Reader (remember to save the PDF when you've finished).

  3. Submit the application form and pay the fee

    What you need to know

    There is a £500 licence fee

    You'll need a debit or credit card

    You'll need to supply the following:

    Premises plan (to scale and clearly displaying the required information)

    Consent form of premises supervisor (only required if the application includes the sale of alcohol)

    Submit application
