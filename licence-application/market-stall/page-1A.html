What you need to know before you apply

You will need a debit or credit card

You will need to submit copies of the following supporting documents

  • Public liability insurance certificate
  • Photo ID (driving licence or passport)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
  • Food hygiene/safety certificate

Apply online

The issuing authority for this licence is London Borough of Westminster (change location).
