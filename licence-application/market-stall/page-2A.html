Apply for a market stall licence
from London Borough of Barnet

Step 1 of 4
  1. Submit application
  2. Email address
  3. Pay licence fee
  4. Done

Step 1: Submit your application

Follow the instructions below:

  1. Download the application form

    Right-click the link below and select the 'Save' option

    Download the application form (PDF)

  2. Fill in the application form on your computer

    To edit the application form you'll need a recent version of Adobe Reader (remember to save the PDF when you've finished).

  3. Submit the application form and any supporting documents

    If you can't complete the form right now don't worry, you can edit it in your own time. Once you're ready, follow the instructions in the email we send you to complete the application.

    Application form

    Please attach the completed application form below.

    Supporting documents

    PDF, Word, TXT, RTF, JPG or PNG format. Photos of documents are accepted, as long as all the relevant information is clear.

    What if I can't submit all of these documents?

    You don't have to submit documents marked 'optional', but if you can it may improve your chances of a successful application.

    If you can't submit the documents digitally you can send copies by post instead. We'll give you the address once you've paid the licence fee.
