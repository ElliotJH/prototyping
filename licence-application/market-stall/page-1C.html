You can apply for this licence online.

Before you apply...

There's a Â£500 annual licence fee for this licence. You'll need a debit or credit card to make the payment. You'll also need to provide copies of the following supporting documentation:

  • Public liability insurance certificate
  • Photo ID (driving licence or passport)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
  • Food hygiene/safety certificate

We recommend you get everything ready before you start the application.
